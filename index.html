Campus Life

What's New

Distance Learning!

One thing that is new about our school is that since the COVID-19 crisis has put all of us into lockdown, we can't go to school on campus anymore! But luckily, we found a solution to our problem, which is online classes from home so that we can keep progressing and learning new things every day! Go raptors!!!

Friday Morning Assemblies Going Digital!

One more great thing that is new about our school is that since we are at home and can't have our traditional Friday morning assembly every Friday, the school is digitally having the assembly by making a video about everything going on around our school! Now, every grade gets to see the video posted every Friday, and it sure is awesome!!!
